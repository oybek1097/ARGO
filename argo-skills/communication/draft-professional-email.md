# Draft a Professional Email

1. Clarify the goal: inform, request, or decide — and the audience.
2. Lead with the ask or key point in the first two sentences.
3. Keep paragraphs short; use bullets for any list of items.
4. State a clear next step and deadline if action is needed.
5. Re-read for tone; remove hedging and ensure it is respectful.
