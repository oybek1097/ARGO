# Write an Incident Status Update

1. State current impact in user terms — what is broken for whom.
2. Give the status: investigating, identified, monitoring, or resolved.
3. Share what is being done, without speculation or blame.
4. Commit to the next update time, and keep that commitment.
5. On resolution, confirm recovery and promise a postmortem.
