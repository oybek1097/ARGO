# Give Constructive Feedback

1. Anchor feedback to specific, observed behaviour or output.
2. Describe the impact rather than labelling the person.
3. Balance what worked with what to change.
4. Make each suggestion concrete and actionable.
5. Invite dialogue — feedback is a conversation, not a verdict.
