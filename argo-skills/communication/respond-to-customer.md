# Respond to a Customer Complaint

1. Acknowledge the issue and the customer's frustration first.
2. Confirm you understand the problem by restating it.
3. Explain what you can do now and a realistic timeline.
4. Avoid jargon, blame, and over-promising.
5. Follow up after resolution to confirm satisfaction.
