# Write a Decision Record

1. State the context and the problem being decided.
2. List the options considered with their trade-offs.
3. Record the decision and the reasoning behind it.
4. Note the consequences, including what becomes harder.
5. Date it and mark its status (proposed, accepted, superseded).
