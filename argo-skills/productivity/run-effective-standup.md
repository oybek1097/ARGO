# Run an Effective Standup

1. Keep it short — focus on progress, plan, and blockers.
2. Have each person speak to the board, not just status.
3. Surface blockers and assign an owner to resolve each.
4. Take detailed discussions offline with the relevant people.
5. End with a clear picture of the day's priorities.
