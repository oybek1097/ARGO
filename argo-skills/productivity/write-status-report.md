# Write a Concise Status Report

1. Lead with overall status: on track, at risk, or blocked.
2. Summarise progress against the plan, not raw activity.
3. Call out risks and what you need from others.
4. State the next concrete milestone and its date.
5. Keep it scannable and free of jargon.
