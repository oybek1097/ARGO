# Prioritise a Backlog

1. Score each item by impact and effort.
2. Surface anything time-sensitive or blocking other work.
3. Promote quick high-impact wins to the top.
4. Defer or drop low-impact, high-effort items explicitly.
5. Re-rank regularly as new information arrives.
