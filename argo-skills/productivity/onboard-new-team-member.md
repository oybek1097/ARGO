# Onboard a New Team Member

1. Prepare access, tools, and accounts before day one.
2. Give a guided tour of the codebase and architecture.
3. Assign a small, real first task with a buddy.
4. Schedule regular check-ins for the first weeks.
5. Collect feedback to improve onboarding for the next person.
