# Ask Effective Clarifying Questions

1. List what you do and do not yet know.
2. Prioritise the questions that most change your approach.
3. Ask specific, answerable questions — avoid vague ones.
4. Offer options when it helps the other person respond.
5. Confirm your understanding by restating the answers.
