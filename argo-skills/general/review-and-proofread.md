# Review and Proofread Text

1. First pass: check structure, logic, and completeness.
2. Second pass: clarity — cut jargon, hedging, and redundancy.
3. Third pass: grammar, spelling, and punctuation.
4. Verify facts, names, numbers, and links.
5. Read it aloud to catch awkward phrasing.
