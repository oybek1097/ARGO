# Plan How to Learn a New Skill

1. Define a concrete, observable goal for what 'learned' means.
2. Break the skill into sub-skills and sequence them.
3. Choose one primary resource to avoid scattering effort.
4. Practise deliberately on real tasks, not just reading.
5. Schedule review and seek feedback to correct mistakes early.
