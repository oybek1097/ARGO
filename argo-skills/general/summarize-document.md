# Summarise a Long Document

1. Identify the document's purpose and intended audience.
2. Extract the main thesis and the key supporting points.
3. Drop examples and repetition; keep the load-bearing claims.
4. Write the summary at a length matched to the reader's need.
5. Preserve any critical caveats, numbers, or deadlines.
