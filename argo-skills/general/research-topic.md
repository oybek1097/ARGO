# Research a Topic Thoroughly

1. Frame the specific question you need answered.
2. Gather sources from multiple independent, credible origins.
3. Cross-check key claims; note where sources disagree.
4. Distinguish established fact from opinion and speculation.
5. Synthesise findings with citations and flag remaining gaps.
