# Assess Feasibility of a Request

1. Clarify exactly what is being asked and the success criteria.
2. Identify the constraints: time, skills, budget, and dependencies.
3. Spot the riskiest unknowns and how to de-risk them quickly.
4. Outline a rough approach and whether it fits the constraints.
5. Give a clear verdict: feasible, feasible-with-changes, or not.
